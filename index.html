Who Am I?

Hi, I’m Jhamiya Jones, a dedicated professional from Atlanta, Georgia, with a passion for growth, creativity, and problem-solving. Above all, I am a proud mother to a beautiful daughter, who inspires me every day to strive for balance, excellence, and self-care in all aspects of life. Family is at the core of everything I do, and I firmly believe that maintaining a sense of harmony between personal and professional ambitions is essential for long-term success.

My background is deeply rooted in human development, education, and organization. With a Bachelor of Science in Human Development and Family Sciences from the University of Georgia, I have cultivated a strong foundation in understanding people, problem-solving, and adaptability. Throughout my career, I have gained experience in childcare, special education, case management, customer service, and operational support—skills that have shaped my ability to work efficiently, think critically, and communicate effectively.

Now, I am expanding my expertise into the world of technology, with a focus on software engineering. I have beginner experience in HTML, CSS, Flexbox, and wireframing, and I am eager to continue developing my skills in web development and programming. My ability to organize, analyze, and create user-friendly experiences makes me a strong candidate for the tech industry.

Skills Relevant to Software Engineering:

  • Problem-solving & critical thinking
  • Attention to detail & organization
  • Adaptability & quick learning
  • Wireframing & UI/UX awareness
  • HTML & CSS (including Flexbox)
  • Communication & collaboration
  • Project management & operational support
